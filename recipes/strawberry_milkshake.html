Yummalicious strawberry milkshake

Description

This fresh strawberry smoothie with vanilla ice cream is great on a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup milk
  • 7 strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • ¼ cup vanilla ice cream
  • ¼ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. In the container of a blender, combine milk, strawberries, ice cream, and sugar. Blend until smooth, about 10 seconds. Pour into a glass and drink with a wide straw.
